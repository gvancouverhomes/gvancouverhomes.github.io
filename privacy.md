# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: August 2025_

## Our Privacy Promise

Welcome to **tanvirbhupal.com**, a private resource for individuals and families exploring real estate opportunities in Surrey and Greater Vancouver.

Privacy, discretion, and trust are fundamental to how I work. Whether you are browsing listings, reading market insights, or reaching out directly, any information you share is treated with care and used only for its intended purpose.

Personal information is never sold, traded, or used for unsolicited marketing.

## Information We Collect

Information may be collected in the following ways:

- Contact details submitted through the contact page or direct email  
- Details you voluntarily share when requesting guidance, listings, or market information  
- Basic, non-identifiable website analytics such as page visits or browser data  

This site does not require account creation to explore content.

## How Information Is Used

Information is used only when relevant and appropriate, including:

- Responding to inquiries related to buying, selling, or relocating within Greater Vancouver  
- Providing tailored real estate guidance, listings, or market context when requested  
- Maintaining website performance, security, and functionality  

There is no mass marketing, mailing list enrollment, or automated outreach.

## Confidentiality in Real Estate Matters

Real estate decisions often involve sensitive financial and personal considerations. Any information shared in the context of a real estate inquiry is handled discreetly and in accordance with applicable Canadian and British Columbia privacy standards.

## Your Choices and Control

You remain in control of your information at all times.

You may request updates or deletion of your information by contacting  
[tanvir@tanvirbhupal.com](mailto:tanvir@tanvirbhupal.com)

You may also manage or disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer additional privacy.

## Third-Party Websites

This site may link to third-party platforms such as listing portals or video services. These external sites operate under their own privacy policies, and this site is not responsible for their practices.

## Data Security and Compliance

Information is handled in accordance with Canadian privacy legislation and British Columbia real estate regulations. Reasonable safeguards are used to protect information from unauthorized access or misuse.

## Privacy FAQ

All information shared is treated as confidential and used only to support requested real estate guidance or communication.

No. Information is not sold or shared for third-party marketing purposes. Disclosure occurs only when legally required.

### Can I request deletion of my information?

Yes. You may request deletion at any time by emailing  
[tanvir@tanvirbhupal.com](mailto:tanvir@tanvirbhupal.com)

Information is managed securely in accordance with applicable Canadian regulations.
