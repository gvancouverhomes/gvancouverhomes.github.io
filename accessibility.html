Our Commitment

At gvancouverhomes.com, we believe everyone deserves full access to Vancouver’s premier real estate insights—no exceptions. Our clients include families, executives, and individuals relocating to Vancouver, and we are committed to serving all with dignity, inclusion, and care.

Accessibility Standards (“WCAG 2.1”)

This website is designed and reviewed to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1—the international standard for digital accessibility. This means our site is built to work with screen readers, keyboard navigation, high contrast settings, and other assistive technologies.
In plain English: WCAG 2.1 ensures our website is usable for everyone—including those with different abilities or preferences for how they browse.

Continuous Improvement

We regularly test our site for accessibility, including text readability, color contrast, alternative text for images, and keyboard-friendly navigation.

How to Request Help or Alternative Formats

If you have difficulty using any part of this website—or need property details or reports in a different format—please reach out. We can provide:

  • Property listings or guides in large font, print, audio, or translated versions
  • Private phone or video consultations
  • In-person tours with accessible accommodations upon request

Accessibility FAQs

What is WCAG 2.1?

WCAG 2.1 stands for Web Content Accessibility Guidelines, version 2.1. It's the international standard for making websites usable for everyone, including those with visual, hearing, or mobility differences.

How quickly will you respond to accessibility requests?

We aim to respond to all accessibility inquiries within 2 business days—your comfort and access are a priority.

Can I request property information in another format?

Yes. Whether you need listings, reports, or contracts in a different format (audio, print, large font, translated), just email or call and we'll provide it promptly.

Feedback

Your feedback makes us better. If you have suggestions or accessibility needs, contact us and we’ll do everything possible to accommodate you.
