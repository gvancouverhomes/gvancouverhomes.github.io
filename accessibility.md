# Accessibility Statement

_Last updated: August 2025_  
_Reviewed annually. Next review: August 2026_

## Commitment

tanvirbhupal.com is committed to providing an accessible experience for all visitors, including people who use assistive technologies such as screen readers, keyboard navigation, or high-contrast settings.

This site is intended to support clients and families exploring real estate in Surrey and Greater Vancouver, and accessibility is treated as part of professional service.

## Standards

This website is designed with reference to the **Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1**.

In practical terms, that means we aim for:
- Clear structure and readable text  
- Meaningful link text and headings  
- Keyboard-friendly navigation  
- Alternative text for images where appropriate  
- Reasonable color contrast

## Accessibility FAQ

### What is WCAG 2.1?

WCAG 2.1 stands for Web Content Accessibility Guidelines, version 2.1. It is a widely used international standard for making websites usable for people with different abilities and browsing needs.

### How quickly will you respond to accessibility requests?

I aim to respond as soon as possible, typically within two business days.

### Can I request real estate information in another format?

Yes. If you need listings, guides, or information shared in a different format, email or call and I will do my best to accommodate promptly.

## Feedback

If you have suggestions or accessibility needs, reach out. Feedback helps improve the experience for everyone.
