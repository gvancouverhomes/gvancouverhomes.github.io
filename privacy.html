Our Privacy Promise

Welcome to gvancouverhomes.com—a private, trusted resource for discerning clients exploring luxury real estate in Greater Vancouver. Your privacy, confidentiality, and trust are fundamental to how we work with families, executives, and clients relocating to Vancouver.

We never sell, trade, or misuse your personal information. Any details you share are used solely to provide you with relevant market insights, property opportunities, and a tailored real estate experience.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details submitted through our contact form or by email
  • Search criteria and preferences when using our MLS search
  • Basic, non-identifiable analytics data (cookies, usage stats)

How Your Information Is Used

  • To respond to your inquiries about exclusive listings or Vancouver market trends

You may request to update or delete your information at any time—just email us. You can also disable cookies in your browser for additional privacy.
